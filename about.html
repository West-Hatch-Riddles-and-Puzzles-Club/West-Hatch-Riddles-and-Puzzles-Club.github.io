About Our Club

The West Hatch Riddle and Puzzle Club is a community for students who enjoy challenging their minds with puzzles, brain teasers, riddles, and logical problems. We foster critical thinking, problem-solving skills, and teamwork through fun puzzling activities.

Our Mission

To create an engaging environment where students can develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills through puzzles and riddles, while building friendships and having fun in the process.

Our Vision

To become a hub of intellectual creativity at West Hatch, where students can explore the fascinating world of puzzles and expand their mental capabilities while enjoying the thrill of solving challenges.

What We Do

Puzzle Workshops

Regular meetings to solve and discuss various types of puzzles and brain teasers.

Competitions

Participate in local and national puzzle competitions as a team.

Escape Room Adventures

Organize and participate in escape room challenges that test problem-solving skills.

Puzzle Creation

Learn to design and create your own puzzles, riddles, and brain teasers.

Join Us

The West Hatch Riddle and Puzzle Club welcomes students of all levels who enjoy mental challenges. Whether you're a seasoned puzzler or just curious about riddles, we'd love to have you join us!
